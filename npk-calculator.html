How It Works

This tool calculates the total weight of fertilizer product needed to hit a specific nutrient target. Simply enter your fertilizer's N-P-K, the area size, and which nutrient you are targeting.

Example: To apply 1 lb of Nitrogen per 1000 sq ft to a 5000 sq ft lawn using a 24-0-6 fertilizer, the calculator determines you need 20.83 lbs of the product.
